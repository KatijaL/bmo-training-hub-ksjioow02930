What we manage

Operations & Clinical Standardization

We reduce variation, enable safe installs, and keep day‑to‑day operations humming across programs.

Our Operations & Clinical Standardization portfolio focuses on harmonizing equipment, workflows, and support services across clinics so staff encounter familiar layouts and reliable tools room‑to‑room. We partner with Biomed, FMO, IPAC/MDRD, and CI/PDHIS to ensure new devices are serviceable, installable, and cleanable. We support readiness checks for power/data, vendor PM models, and surface/material compatibility, and we maintain the SOPs that keep devices safe and dependable throughout their life.

Capital Requests &
Prioritization

From intake to funding, we run the annual capital cycle and urgent requests with clear quality gates.

We guide requests from idea → vetted need → funded. Using SPO, we collect vendor packages (quotes/specs/warranty/service/consumables/Health Canada), route Support Services approvals, confirm leadership endorsement, and prepare submissions to Foundations and MoH.

See more ▾

Procurement & Project Management

Clean Cap/ePro requisitions, timely POs, coordinated installs, and invoice reconciliation to close the loop.

Once funded, we finalize quotes and coding, raise Cap/ePro requisitions, and hand off for PO issuance. Post‑PO, we track shipments, stage receiving, book Biomed inspection and FMO readiness, schedule in‑service, and manage deficiencies. We reconcile invoices in Basware to PO lines and funding letters.
