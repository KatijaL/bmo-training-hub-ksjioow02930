FAQ

How do I request a new capital item?

Submit a new entry in SPO’s Request Intake; you’ll receive a CWBMO# with links to Checklist, Engagement, and Documentation.

When is an RFP required?

At or above competitive thresholds and when best‑value evaluation applies.

What does “not on contract” mean?

No active agreement; compete or justify exemption.

Where can I see project status?

Use SPO → Grand View; PO details in PeopleSoft; invoices in Basware.

What is Prior‑Year Contingency?

Unspent Annual Grant carried forward to buffer shortfalls or urgent needs.

How do I split funding across items?

Use correct project codes and ePro distribution splits; mirror in SPO mapping.

Do trials require extra steps?

Yes—Health Canada licensing, trial agreement, Biomed intake, IPAC/MDRD cleaning, and Privacy/IT if data/networking.

What if a request is incomplete?

It remains in Vetting until required checklist items are provided and uploaded.

What does PM mean?

Preventive maintenance—scheduled inspections and service to keep equipment safe and reliable.
